Our Menu

Not sure what to order? We've got you covered. Enjoy our all-day breakfast menu with a wide variety of options. We recommend you trying Breakfast Veggie Bowl, Soft Scramble, or The Bacon Omelette. Each served with home fries and toast. We also do breakfast sandwiches, like the Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Breakfast Sandwich, Grilled Cheese Breakfast Sandwich, or the Bacon and Tomato Breakfast Sandwich. All sandwiches are served with your choice of white, whole wheat, or rye bread. You can also try our Oh-so-delicious smoothies bowl packed with fresh fruits and other super foods!
